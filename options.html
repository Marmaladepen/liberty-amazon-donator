Purpose: Liberty Amazon Donator is a Chrome browser extension that makes it easy to direct a portion of the money you spend on Amazon to your favorite Liberty organization at no cost to you! Once the extension is installed, it will add an Amazon Associate Id tag to the Amazon links you visit and your organization will receive a kickback for every purchase you make.

Usage: By default, every 24 hours the extension picks a random organization to donate to (since the Amazon cookie is active for 24 hours). For the next 24 hours, Amazon assigns a share of any purchases you make to this organization (between 1% to 10% capped to $10-$25 maximum depending).

You can add or remove an organization as well as update its affiliate codes per country.

If you'd prefer to give a particular organization more (or less) chances of a getting a share of your purchases, then you can increase (or decrease) its assigned probability to any value between 0% (will never be picked) and 100% (will always be picked).

Implementation: This extension is a rewritten version of the Amazon Affiliate Link extension which itself is a stripped down version of the Philanthropist extension. The source code for this extension is maintained at https://github.com/Marmaladepen/liberty-amazon-donator. Contact: marmalade.pen@gmail.com
